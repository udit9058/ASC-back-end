SMART EDUCATION(AICTE),SMART AUTOMATION

AICTE approved courses in the field of engineering and technology,management,pharmacy, architecture etc are affiliated to different universities and are conducted as per the syllabus prescribed by these universities.there are more than 100 such universities in the country afiliating the same course(s). however,the structure of the course,content and scheme of the course differs widely from university to university. resulting in varition in the academic standard of the same degree approved by AICTE at the time of admission of higher studies and employment in india and abroad.

Standard Course Curriculum design for all the universities across india

Therefore there is a need to develop an application to map stucture,content and scheme of the course at present run by affiliated universities and map important features and compare with a standard cirriculum of the highest standard .this would help AICTE to devlop and enforce uniform academic standards all over the country for all its approved courses.
